Email Alias Generator

Arcunox reduces your digital footprint by generating safe, disposable identities you can use instead of your real information. Everything runs locally in your browser. No accounts. No tracking. No uploads. No storage.

Many attacks start with small pieces of exposed data: an email reused across sites, a predictable username, a weak password, or a leaked identifier. Once these pieces can be linked together, they form a profile that scammers and automated systems can target.

Arcunox tools break that link. Each tool creates clean, consistent, single-purpose identities that keep your real information out of circulation and reduce what attackers, data brokers, and automated systems can exploit.
